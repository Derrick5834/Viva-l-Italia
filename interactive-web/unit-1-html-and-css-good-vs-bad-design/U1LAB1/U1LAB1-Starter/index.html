About Deltarune

Deltarune is an episodic role-playing video game by indie developer Toby Fox. Set in a parallel universe to Fox's previous game, Undertale, the game follows Kris, a human teenager destined to save the world alongside Susie, a monster, and Ralsei, a prince from the Dark World. During their journey, the group forms relationships with a variety of allies and enemies.
